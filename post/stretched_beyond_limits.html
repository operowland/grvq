It takes the Spirit to guide you into all truths. As the Spirit guide you into the truths, your eyes will see, ears will hear and mind will conceive those things God has prepared in advance even for you. To be guided into these truths call for some stretching.

The person to stretch is not the physical you, because the real you is not the person you see in the mirror. You have known that man is a spirit with a soul living in a body. Until you stretch your spirit, you will not get to your full potential in life, as the flow is from the spirit to the soul and not vice versa. 

When you are stretched, your understanding changes. You know that you are more than the person you see now and can certainly be more and do more in Christ, through Christ and for Christ.
